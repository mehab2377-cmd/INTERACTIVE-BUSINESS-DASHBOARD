### 🎯 Problem & Solution
Integrated complex E-commerce datasets to provide a centralized view of Sales, Orders, and Product performance.

### 🛠 Tools & Technologies
* **Power BI Desktop:** For building the interactive visualization layer.
* **Power Query (ETL):** Used for data cleaning and transformation.
* **DAX (Calculated Measures):** Created advanced measures for key business metrics.
* **Data Modeling (Star Schema):** Designed an optimized data structure for fast and accurate reporting.

### 💡 Business Impact
Empowered management to identify high-performing categories and optimize inventory allocation based on real-time sales trends.
